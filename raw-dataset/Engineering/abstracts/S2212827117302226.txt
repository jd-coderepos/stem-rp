Thanks to the information revolution of the late 20th century, railway operators have hada chance to realize innovative change in railway maintenance. The Research and Development Center of the JR Group has proposed the “Smart Maintenance Initiative” as a 21st-century innovation for railway facility maintenance. This paper describes the overview of the “Smart Maintenance Initiative” and demonstrates actual developments for track maintenance, such as high-frequency monitoring device and analyzing methods and future prospects, implementing Condition-Based Maintenance (CBM) which is one of the key parts of the Smart Maintenance Initiative.
