Title of referenced paper Solar energy at birth and human lifespan, Journal of Photochemistry & Photobiology B 186 (2018)59–68. This paper uses National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS) death data collected from 1979- 2013, inclusive, and average monthly solar intensity as measured by sunspot number collected from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) from 1900–2013, inclusive.
