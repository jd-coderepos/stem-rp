This dataset presents a series of broad Red and Near-Infrared (NIR) bands obtained from consumer-grade camera in estimating chlorophyll contents in Robusta coffee (Coffea Canephora) plants at the leaf level. A total of 600 leaves were measured using direct-leaf tools namely SPAD-502 chlorophyll meter, Spectrometer, and modified consumer-grade camera using a 665 nm long-pass external filter.
