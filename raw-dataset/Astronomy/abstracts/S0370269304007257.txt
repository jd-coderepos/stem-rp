We study possible anomalous top-quark couplings generated by SU(2)×U(1) gauge-invariant dimension-6 effective operators, using the final b-quark momentum distribution in γγ→t t ̄ →bX. Taking into account non-standard t t ̄ γ, tbW and γγH couplings, we perform an optimal-observable analysis in order to estimate the precision for the determination of all relevant non-standard couplings.
