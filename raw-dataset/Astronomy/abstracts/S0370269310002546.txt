B → ( π , η , η ′ ) transition form factors are investigated in the covariant light-front approach. With theoretical uncertainties, we find that B → ( π , η , η ′ ) form factors at q 2 = 0 are f + ( π , η , η ′ ) ( 0 ) = ( 0.245 − 0.001 + 0.000 ± 0.011 , 0.220 ± 0.009 ± 0.009 , 0.180 ± 0.008 − 0.007 + 0.008 ) for vector current and f T ( π , η , η ′ ) ( 0 ) = ( 0.239 − 0.003 − 0.018 + 0.002 + 0.020 , 0.211 ± 0.009 − 0.015 + 0.017 , 0.173 ± 0.007 − 0.013 + 0.014 ) for tensor current, respectively. With the obtained q 2 -dependent f + π ( q 2 ) and observed branching ratio (BR) for B ¯ d → π + ℓ ν ¯ ℓ , the V u b is found as | V u b | L F = ( 3.99 ± 0.13 ) × 10 − 3 .
