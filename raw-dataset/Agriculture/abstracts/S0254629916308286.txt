Cylindrocladium reteaudii (Bugn.) Boesew. is recognized as the correct name for C. hederae Arnaud ex Peerally and C. leucothoes El-Gholl, Leahy & Schubert, which are regarded as synonyms. Calonectria hederae Booth & Murray is reduced to a synonym of Cal. reteaudii (Bugn.) Booth. Each species is described and illustrated.
