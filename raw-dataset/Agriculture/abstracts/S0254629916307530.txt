The Ac land type in the Newcastle – Memel – Chelmsford Dam area forms the foothills of the Low Drakensberg, consists mainly of dolerite, and is situated between the Bb and the Fa land types in the study area. The results of the numerical classification (Twinspan) applied to 39 relevés compiled in this land type were further refined by Braun-Blanquet procedures. Two main types of grassland were identified, as well as limited hygrophilous communities and a woodland community. The grasslands represent four major communities and 15 subcommunities. The plant communities were hierarchically classified, described and ecologically interpreted. Die Ac-landtipe in die Newcastle – Memel – Chelmsforddamgebied vorm die voetheuwels van die Lae Drakensberg en geologies bestaan dit hoofsaaklik uit doleriet. Dit is tussen die Bb-en Fa-landtipes in die studiegebied gelee. Die resultate van ’n numeriese klassifikasie (Twinspan) van 39 relevés is met behulp van Braun-Blanquetprosedures verder verfyn. Twee hoofgrasveldtipes is geidentifiseer, sowel as higrofiele gemeenskappe en ’n boomgemeenskap van beperkte omvang. Die twee hoofgrasveldtipes word verteen-woordig deur vier hoofgemeenskappe wat verder in 15 subgemeenskappeonderverdeel is. Die plantgemeen-skappe is hiërargies geklassifiseer, beskryf en ekologies geïnterpreteer.
