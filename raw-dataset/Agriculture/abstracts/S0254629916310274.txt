During surveys of fungi occurring on Eucalyptus, five leaf-inhabiting fungi, previously unknown in South Africa, were found. These include Clypeophysalospora latitans (Sacc.) Swart, Codinaea septata Sutton & Hodges, Cytospora australiae Speg., ldiocercus australis (Cooke) Swart and Seimatosporium eucalypti (Mc Alp.) Swart.
