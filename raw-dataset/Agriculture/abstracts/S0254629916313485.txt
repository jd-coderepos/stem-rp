The genus Dichilus DC. comprises five distinct species, namely D. gracilis Eckl. & Zeyh., D. lebeckioides DC., D. pilosus Conrath ex Schinz, D. strictus E. Mey. and D. reflexus (N.E. Br.) A.L. Schutte. The nomenclature, complete synonymy and typification, as well as a short diagnosis and the geographical distribution of each species are given.
