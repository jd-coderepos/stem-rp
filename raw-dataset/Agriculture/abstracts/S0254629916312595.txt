Three new species of Ixia from the south-western Cape Province are described. They are I. frederickii De Vos and I. mostertii De Vos, both falling under section Ixia, and I. esterhuyseniae De Vos of section Morphixia. I. frederickii has close affinities with I. maculata L. and I. dubia Vent., I. mostertii is allied to I. vinacea G.J. Lewis, and I. esterhuyseniae is closely allied to I. odorata Ker-Gawler.
