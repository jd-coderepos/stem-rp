Two new species of Pelargonium L’ Hérit. (Geraniaceae) are described from South Africa. P. tongaense Vorster. from north-eastern Natal, is placed in the section Ciconium (Sweet) Harv. on account of its floral morphology. P. citronellum J.J.A. v.d. Walt of the section Pelargonium, from the southern Cape Province, has previously been misidentified as P. scabrum (Burm. f.) L’ Hérit. For each species a plate of illustrations and a distribution map are provided.
