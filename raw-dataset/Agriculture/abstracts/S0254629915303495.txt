Raphionacme Harv. is a genus of mostly geophytic herbaceous plants, numbering 37 species and, with the exception of one species, all endemic to Africa. In southern Africa (Botswana, Lesotho, Namibia, South Africa and Swaziland) a total of 16 species are recognised by the present author, although no less than 27 species and two varieties were described for Raphionacme in the region. Six species and two varietietal names are new synonyms for the region. These names are synonymised here.
