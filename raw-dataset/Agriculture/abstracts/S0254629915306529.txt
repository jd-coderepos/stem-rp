Passalora dodonaeae Crous & U. Braun is newly described from leaves of Dodonaea angustifolia L. from South Africa. Type material of Cercospora mitteriana Syd. has been re-examined and this species reduced to synonymy with Pseudocercospora mitteriana Goh & W.H. Hsieh. Pseudocercospora dodonaeae Boesew. is also discussed, based on the re-examination of type material.
