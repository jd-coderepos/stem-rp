The Afromontane fynbos communities of Korannaberg were classified using Twinspan numerical analysis and refined by the Braun-Blanquet technique. The Passerina montana - Cymbopogon dieterlenii fynbos was divided into two communities, of which one was divided into two sub-communities. Descriptions of the plant communities include diagnostic species and the habitat features such as geology, altitude, aspect, slope and soil factors such as soil type, depth and rockiness of the soil surface.
