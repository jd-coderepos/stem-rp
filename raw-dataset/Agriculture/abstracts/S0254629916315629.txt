A new combination, Mariscus solidus (Kunth) Vorster is made, and the basionym is lectotypified. This is the correct name for what was previously known as Mariscus gueinzii C. B. Cl. and Cyperus dactyliformis Boeck. Mariscus thunbergii (Vahl) Schrad. is shown to be the correct name for what was formerly known as Mariscus riparius Schrad. and Cyperus thunbergii Vahl.
