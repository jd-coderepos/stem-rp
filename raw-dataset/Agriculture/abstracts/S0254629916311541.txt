During the collection and study of the fungal flora of South Africa four new Ascomycete records were encountered: Melomastia mastoidea (Fr.) Schroeter (Sphaeriales: genus and species record); Herpotrichia schiedermayeriana Fckl., Didymosphaeria striatula P. et Sacc. and Dothidea puccinioides (DC.) Fr. (Dothideales: species records). The specimens are described and illustrated.
