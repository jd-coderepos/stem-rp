Batcheloromyces leucadendri Van Wyk, Marasas & Knox-Davies sp. nov., is described and illustrated from leaves of several species of Leucadendron (Proteaceae) in the Cape Province of the Republic of South Africa. In the new species sporodochium-like plates are smaller, the conidia are larger with more septa and they are more rough-walled than in the closely related B. proteae Marasas, Van Wyk & Knox-Davies on Protea spp. S. Air. J. Bot. 1985, 51: 344–346 Batcheloromyces leucadendri Van Wyk, Marasas & Knox-Davies sp. nov., word beskryf en geïllustreer op blare van Leucadendron (Proteaceae) in die Kaapprovinsie, Republiek van Suid-Afrika. Die nuwe spesie het kleiner sporodochium-agtige plate, groter conidia met meer septa en ‘n growwer tekstuur as die van die naverwante B. proteae Marasas, Van Wyk & Knox-Davies op Protea spp. S.-Afr. Tydskr. Plantk. 1985, 51: 344–346
