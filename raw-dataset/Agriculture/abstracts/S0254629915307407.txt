The history of Tetraria bachmannii Kükenthal is given. Reasons are provided for the transfer of this species to Cyathocoma Nees as C. bachmannii (Kükenthal) C. Archer, which is redescribed and illustrated. A recircumscription of the southern African endemic genus Cyathocoma and a key to its three species is included.
