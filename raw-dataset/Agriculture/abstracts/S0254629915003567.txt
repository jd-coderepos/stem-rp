The Southern African species of Gymnanthemum Cass. (Asteraceae: Vernonieae) are revised. Eight species are recognised from the region, with full descriptions, distribution maps, and a key to the species. The new combination G. capense (A. Spreng.) J. C. Manning and N. Swelankomo is provided for the species previously treated as G. mespilifolium (Less.) H. Rob.
