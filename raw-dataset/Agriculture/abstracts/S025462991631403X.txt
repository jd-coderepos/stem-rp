The nomenclature and taxonomy of Argyrolobium variopile N.E. Br. has been studied. Morphologically the species resembles species of Dichilus DC. and Lotononis (DC.) Eckl. & Zeyh., but its taxonomic position in Argyrolobium is confirmed. It is also shown that Lotononis magnistipulata Duemmer is a synonym of this species.
