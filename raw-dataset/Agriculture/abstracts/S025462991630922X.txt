A re-evaluation of the monotypic genus Nylandtia Dumort. is presented in which two varieties, N. spinosa (L.) Dumort. var. spinosa and var. scoparia (Eckl. & Zeyh.) Johnson & Weitz, are recognized. Descriptions, synonymy, distribution data, and a key to the varieties are provided. Variation in the two varieties is covered and pollination ecology is discussed.
