Three new species from the semi-desert parts of southern Africa are described: Adromischus subdistichus (Crassulaceae) from the south-eastern Cape, Senecio laticipes (Asteraceae) from the northern Knersvlakte and Tylecodon stenocaulis (Crassulaceae) from the south-western corner of the Ceres Karoo. Tylecodon pygmaeus var. tenuis Tölken from the Knersvlakte is raised to species level as Tylecodon tenuis (Tölken) Bruyns. Keys are provided to distinguish the new species of Senecio and Tolecodon.
