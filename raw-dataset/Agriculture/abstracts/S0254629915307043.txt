The fungus Nitschkia broomeiana (Berk.) Nannf. is reported from South Africa for the first time, Only one other species of this genus has previously been recorded here: N. confertula (Schw.) Nannf. [as syn. Tympanopsis euomphala (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Stalb.]. These are the only two representatives of the family Nitschkiaceae, as delimited at present, reported from this country. The fungus is described, illustrated, and compared with other Nitschkia species.
