The taxonomic history, position within the subfamily Papilionoideae and morphological variation of the genus Virgilia Poir. are briefly discussed. Three taxa, namely V. oroboides (Berg.) Salter subsp. oroboides, V. oroboides (Berg.) Salter subsp. ferruginea B.-E. van Wyk subsp. nov. and V. divaricata Adamson are described. Keys to the species and subspecies are provided and the natural distribution given.
