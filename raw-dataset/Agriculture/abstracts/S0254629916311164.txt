A taxonomic revision of Tacazzea Decne. is presented. Four species out of a total of 17 species and three varieties described are maintained. T. apiculata Oliv., T. conferta N.E. Br., T. rosmarinifolia (Decne.) N.E. Br. and T. venosa Decne. are recognized. Most of the synonyms belong to the widely distributed, polymorphic T. apiculata. Tacazzea is endemic to and widely distributed in Africa. All four species inhabit moist habitats of river banks. Two species are lianas, the other two are shrubs.
