Nomenclatural anomalies in Dicliptera Juss. and Adhatoda Miller are explained and new combinations in Peristrophe Nees and Adhatoda proposed. Duvernoia tridnocalyx Lindau is placed in synonymy with Ruellia ovata Thunb. Onreëlmatighede in die nomenklatuur van Dicliptera Juss. en Adhatoda Miller word verklaar en nuwe kombinasies in Peristrophie Neesd en Adhatoda word voorgestel. Duvernoia trichocalyx Lindau word as ‘n sinoniem van Ruellia ovata Thunb. beskou.
