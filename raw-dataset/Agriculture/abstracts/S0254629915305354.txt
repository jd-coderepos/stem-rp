Leaf material and callus cultures of Schlechterina mitostemmatoides (Passifloraceae), an endangered species from northern KwaZulu-Natal, was screened for cyanogenic glycosides. Both leaf and callus material tested positive, Extracts from both sources were further investigated by thin-layer chromatography. This Indicated that the callus culture produced the same cyanogenic compound as the intact plant. Blaarmaterial en kalluskulture van Schlechterina mitostemmatoides (Passifloraceae), 'n bedreigde spesle van die noordelike deel van KwaZulu-Natal, is ondersoek vir sianoglikosiede. Beide blaar- en kallusmaterial het positief getoets. Ekstrakte van die twee bronne is verder ondersoek met behulp van dunlaagchromatografie. Dit het getoon dat kalluskulture dieselfde sianoglikosied as die plant geproduseer het.
