Six new species of African Restionaceae are described from the southern and western Cape Province of South Africa: Thamnochortus karooica Linder, T. amoena Linder, Cannomois taylorii Linder, Calopsis sparsa Esterhuysen, Anthochortus singularis Esterhuysen and Ischyrolepis saxatilis Esterhuysen. Where relevant, anatomical information on the new species is given and brief comments on the distribution and ecology are made.
