The Garden occupies an area of 104ha on the north-western border of Roodepoort. A checklist has been compiled containing 556 plant species. The plant species represent 352 genera and 95 families of which 8 families (8,51 % of the total number) belong to the Pteridophyta, 12 (12,76%) to the Monocotyledoneae and 75 (77,89%) to the Dicotyledoneae. An analysis of floristic data is presented and some comparisons with the flora of various nature reserves are made.
