The identity of the little-known and confusing species, Pelargonium worcesterae Knuth, is established. In addition to a complete description and illustration, anatomical, palynological, karyological and geographical data are supplied. The similarities and differences between P. worcesterae and P. dichondrifolium DC. are discussed. P. worcesterae is placed in the section Ligularia (Sweet) Harv. due to similarities to P. exhibens Vorster.
