The polymorphic species Pelargonium reniforme Curt, is subdivided into subsp. reniforme and subsp. velutinum (Eckl. & Zeyh.) Dreyer on the grounds of morphological differences and allopatric geographical distributions. Illustrations of the subspecies and detailed distribution maps are provided. Die polimorfiese spesie Pelargonium reniforme Curt, word onderverdeel in subsp. reniforme en subsp. velutinum (Eckl. & Zeyh.) Dreyer op grond van morfologiese verskille en allopatriese verspreidings. Illustrasies van die subspesies en gedetailleerde verspreidingskaarte word voorsien.
